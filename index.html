About Me

Adam Payne is a web designer, film producer and editor, motion graphics designer, and improv actor based out of Brooklyn New York. He is a graduate of Yale University and Columbia University's Coding Bootcamp, the former of which he graduated from as the recipient of The Philip C. Lustman Grant for Artistic Accomplishment. He's worked as a producer, designer, and editor for the well-respected music tutorial website Cracking the Code for over 10 years. He's also been performing in several shows at the Magnet Theater for the past five years. And while he can write a serious bio when absolutely necessary, he tries not to take life too seriously in the long run.
